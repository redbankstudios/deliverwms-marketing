PRIVACY POLICY
================================================================

URL: https://deliverwms.com/legal/privacy

WHAT WE COLLECT:
- Account information: name, work email, company name, password (hashed),
  role, optional phone number.
- Customer warehouse data: anything you upload (tenants, products, orders,
  inventory, billable events, etc.). Processed on your behalf under the
  MSA and DPA. Your data, not ours.
- Billing information: billing contact, billing address, last 4 digits of
  payment card (full card numbers handled by Stripe).
- Communications: support tickets, contact form, email content.
- Marketing preferences (only if you opt in).
- Log and usage data: IP, browser, device, sessions, errors.
- Cookies (strictly necessary, performance/analytics, preferences). No
  cross-site advertising tracking.
- Information from authentication providers and Stripe.

HOW WE USE IT:
Provide and operate the Service, authenticate users, process payments,
respond to support, send service announcements, detect fraud, comply with
law, send opt-in marketing.

WHAT WE DON'T DO:
- We do NOT sell personal information.
- We do NOT use customer warehouse data to train AI models without
  explicit, separate, written authorization from the customer.

SUB-PROCESSORS (vetted third-party service providers):
- Vercel Inc. — Web hosting, CDN
- Supabase (or successor PostgreSQL provider) — Application database
- Stripe, Inc. — Payment processing
- Postmark (or successor email service) — Transactional email
- Tawk.to — Live chat widget on marketing site

LEGAL DISCLOSURE: We disclose information when required by subpoena/court
order, to enforce Terms, to investigate fraud or security incidents, or to
protect rights/safety. When permitted, we notify affected customers before
responding to a government request.

DATA RETENTION:
- Account information: while active + up to 90 days after termination.
- Customer warehouse data: while subscription active; deleted within 30
  days post-termination unless contract or law requires longer.
- Billing records: 7 years for tax/accounting.
- Communications: up to 3 years.
- Logs: up to 12 months.

SECURITY: Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, role-based access
controls, multi-factor authentication on admin access, multi-tenant
isolation via database-level row-level security, regular access reviews,
incident response process. Affected customers notified of confirmed
security incidents without undue delay.

USER RIGHTS (CCPA — California residents):
- Know what's collected/used/shared
- Access a copy of personal information
- Delete personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Limit use of sensitive information
- Opt out of sale/sharing (we don't sell or share)
- Non-discrimination for exercising rights

To exercise rights: email privacy@deliverwms.com. Response within 45 days.

OTHER US STATE LAWS: VA, CO, CT, UT, TX residents have similar rights —
honored using the same contact method.

END CUSTOMERS OF DELIVER WMS CUSTOMERS: If a 3PL uses the Service to
process information about you, that 3PL is the data controller. Contact
them directly to exercise rights. We assist as needed.

DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)
================================================================

URL: https://deliverwms.com/legal/dpa

PURPOSE: Governs Deliver WMS's processing of personal information on behalf
of Customer. Incorporated into MSA and Terms by reference.

ROLES:
- Customer is Controller (or "Business" under CCPA).
- Deliver WMS is Processor (or "Service Provider" under CCPA).
- Where Customer is itself acting as a processor for a third-party
  controller (e.g., 3PL processing personal info of its warehouse clients'
  end customers), Deliver WMS acts as a sub-processor.

SCOPE (Section 3):
- Subject matter: providing/securing/improving the Service per Agreement
  and Customer's documented instructions.
- Duration: while Agreement active, plus any post-termination retention.
- Nature/purpose: warehouse management, last-mile delivery, billing,
  reporting per Agreement.
- Categories of Data Subjects: Customer personnel/authorized users,
  personnel of Customer's warehouse clients and their end customers,
  drivers and delivery recipients, other individuals Customer uploads.
- Categories of Personal Information: names, emails, phone, addresses
  (business and delivery), IP addresses, device IDs, transaction/order
  details, signatures, proof-of-delivery photographs and metadata.
- Customer should avoid uploading special-category data (health, biometric,
  precise geolocation) unless strictly necessary.

CUSTOMER'S INSTRUCTIONS (Section 4): Deliver WMS processes only on
Customer's documented instructions (including Service use and
configuration). Will notify if instruction appears to violate law.

SECURITY MEASURES (Section 7):
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest
- Role-based access controls, least-privilege provisioning, MFA for admin
  access, periodic access reviews
- Multi-tenant isolation via database-level row-level security
- Web application firewall, intrusion detection, DDoS mitigation
- Vulnerability scanning, dependency monitoring, timely patching
- Audit logging
- Encrypted backups with documented restore procedures
- Personnel: background checks where lawful, confidentiality obligations,
  security awareness training
- Incident response process
- Code review, automated testing, controlled deployment

SUB-PROCESSORS (Section 8):
Current list:
- Vercel Inc. — Web hosting, CDN (United States)
- Supabase (or successor PostgreSQL provider) — Application database
  (United States)
- Stripe, Inc. — Payment processing (United States)
- Postmark (or successor email service) — Transactional email
  (United States)
- Tawk.to — Live chat widget on marketing site (United States / Cyprus)

30-day advance notice for new sub-processors. Customer can object on
reasonable data-protection grounds; if unresolved, may terminate Order
Form without refund of pre-paid unused fees.

DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS (Section 9): Deliver WMS provides reasonable assistance
to enable Customer to respond to Data Subject rights requests. If Deliver
WMS receives a request directly, will forward to Customer (unless prohibited
by law) and not respond directly except to acknowledge receipt.

PERSONAL DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION (Section 10): Notification without undue
delay (within 72 hours where feasible), with description of breach,
likely consequences, and mitigation. Cooperation with Customer's
investigation.

DATA PROTECTION IMPACT ASSESSMENTS (Section 11): Reasonable assistance to
Customer for DPIAs and regulatory consultations.

INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS (Section 12): Service provided from United States.
Personal information processed in US. DPA does not currently include EU
GDPR Standard Contractual Clauses or UK SCCs — contact
privacy@deliverwms.com before uploading personal data from those
jurisdictions.

CCPA-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS (Section 13):
- Deliver WMS acts as Service Provider.
- Will NOT sell or share Customer Personal Information.
- Will NOT retain/use/disclose outside direct business relationship except
  as permitted by CCPA.
- Will NOT combine with personal information from other customers except as
  permitted.

AUDIT RIGHTS (Section 14):
- Annual right to summary information (e.g., third-party security
  assessment report when available) under confidentiality obligations.
- On-site audit available with 30 days notice and legitimate cause, subject
  to reasonable scope and confidentiality. Customer bears costs unless
  material breach found.

RETURN AND DELETION (Section 15): 30-day export window post-termination.
Deletion or anonymization on Customer's written request, except where
legally required to retain.
