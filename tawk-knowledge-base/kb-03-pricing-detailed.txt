================================================================
GUIDING PRINCIPLES OF DELIVER WMS PRICING
================================================================

1. PUBLISHED, NOT HIDDEN. All tier prices and per-event rates are public.
   No "Contact Us for pricing" gate. Most WMS vendors hide pricing — we don't.

2. PLATFORM TIER + USAGE, NOT PER USER. All plans include unlimited workforce
   seats. Pricing scales with the value you get (orders processed), not with
   how many warehouse staff you happen to have.

3. SAME PER-EVENT RATES ACROSS EVERY PLAN. $0.12 per stop, $0.12 per label,
   $0.18 per pallet position per month — whether you're on PAYG or Scale.

4. NO SETUP FEES. Whatever you sign up for is what you pay.

5. SWITCH PLANS ANYTIME. Upgrades immediate and pro-rated. Downgrades take
   effect at the end of the current billing cycle so you keep what you paid for.

SAVINGS CALCULATOR FRAMING
================================================================

A live savings calculator lives on the pricing page at
https://deliverwms.com/pricing. It takes inputs:

  - Monthly orders
  - Active B2B clients
  - Returns / month
  - Warehouse staff count

And estimates monthly operational time savings across:

  - Receiving (scan-driven vs spreadsheet entry)
  - B2B portal support (self-serve vs email/phone)
  - Billing reconciliation (event-stream vs month-end reconstruction)
  - Returns processing (disposition-aware vs manual)
  - Cycle counts (manager approval gate vs ad-hoc)

For most mid-size 3PLs (200-1,000 monthly orders, 3-10 active clients), the
calculator shows estimated savings well above the platform fee. For example,
typical Starter-tier-fit operations see ~$800-$1,800/month in operational
time savings at fully-loaded labor rates — multiple times the $299/month
Starter fee.

The calculator estimates are based on process design benchmarks, not hard
guarantees. Actual savings depend on the operator's current workflow and
labor cost basis.

PRICING FAQ
================================================================

Q: How are orders counted?
A: An order is one outbound order processed through fulfillment. Plan
allowances reset monthly. Overage rates per plan are listed on each plan
card.

Q: Can I switch plans later?
A: Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are pro-rated. Downgrades take
effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

Q: Do you charge per user?
A: No. All plans include unlimited workforce seats. Pricing is by platform
tier and usage, not per user.

Q: What about Shopify and other integrations?
A: Native marketplace integrations (Shopify, Amazon, WooCommerce, TikTok)
are on the roadmap. The order-import API and webhook events are available
today for custom connections.

Q: Is data isolated per tenant?
A: Yes. Multi-tenant isolation is enforced at the database level via row-
level security from day one.

Q: Are there any setup fees?
A: No. No setup fees on any plan.

Q: Is the platform multi-warehouse ready?
A: All features are available across plans, but multi-warehouse allocation
is a Scale-tier feature. Other plans support a single warehouse per tenant.

Q: How do per-event rates compare to my current carrier costs?
A: The per-event rates ($0.12/stop, $0.12/label, $0.18/pallet/month) are
platform fees only — they do NOT include carrier costs. Your carrier
relationships (UPS, FedEx, USPS, regional carriers) remain separate. The
platform fee is for the orchestration: routing, label generation,
tracking, and audit trail.

Q: What happens if I exceed my included orders for the month?
A: You're billed at the overage rate for that plan:
  - Starter: $0.08 per order over 1,000
  - Growth: $0.06 per order over 5,000
  - Scale: $0.04 per order over 15,000
  PAYG has no included orders — every order is billed at usage rates.

Q: Does the price include support?
A: Yes. PAYG and Starter include email support. Growth includes priority
support (faster response times). Scale includes SLA-backed support with
guaranteed response windows.

Q: How do you bill — monthly or annual?
A: Monthly by default. Annual contracts are available for Scale-tier
operations through a custom agreement — talk to Michael.

Q: How do payments work?
A: Your card on file is charged automatically through Stripe. Platform fee
billed monthly in advance; usage charges billed in arrears at month-end.
If a charge fails, the system retries on a standard schedule and emails you
before any service impact.

Q: Can I get an invoice for my accounting?
A: Yes. Every charge generates an invoice you can download from the Account
Management screen. Standard CSV exports available for any reporting period.

Q: What currency is pricing in?
A: USD. Multi-currency invoicing is on the roadmap for international 3PLs.

Q: Can I get a discount for committing to a longer term?
A: For Scale-tier operations on an annual commitment, discounts are
available through a custom agreement. Talk to Michael for details.

Q: What if my volume varies seasonally?
A: PAYG is built for variable volume — no platform fee, just per-event rates.
Starter/Growth/Scale are good fits for operations with steady baseline volume
plus seasonal spikes (the spikes flow through overage rates).

Q: How does pricing work for the B2B client portal?
A: It's included in your plan. PAYG covers 1 client portal, Starter 3,
Growth and Scale unlimited. There is no per-client portal fee on Growth
or Scale.

Q: Is there a free trial period for paid plans?
A: The Free PAYG tier serves the same purpose — a working free account where
you can validate fit before paying a platform fee. There is no separate "free
trial period" on paid plans because the free tier is always available.
